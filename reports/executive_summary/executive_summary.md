# Executive Summary: FactoryGuard 6G Fleet Risk Analysis

## Project Objectives & Overview
FactoryGuard 6G is an industrial decision-support system deployed in the smart manufacturing facility to analyze machine-level sensor telemetry alongside 6G network communication metrics. Its purpose is to prioritize maintenance dispatches by identifying statistical sensor anomalies and predicting future operational deterioration.

## Production Model Highlight
Following temporal cross-validation, the **Calibrated XGBoost Classifier** was selected as the production model, achieving the highest test **PR-AUC (0.088)** compared to logistic regression baselines. The model’s predictions are fused with Isolation Forest anomaly scores to compute the final 0–100 Fused Risk Score.
